Welcome to YouTube Stream Finder

YouTube Stream Finder is a tool to better locate YouTube streamer's channels!

Users

  • Head to App to find and save your favorite streams!

Developers

Here are the YouTube Stream Finder API endpoints:

  • /search?term="fortnite"
  • /getStreams

The first endpoint retrieves streams from YouTube with the search term provided.
The second endpoint retrives channels that have been favorited within the YouTube Stream Finder app.
